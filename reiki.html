Introduction to Reiki Energy Healing

Reiki is the one of the energy healing modality developed by Dr. Mikao Usui in which healer becomes the medium of universal healing energy and other person to be healed. It is composite of two Japanese words: “Rei” and “Ki”. Rei means universal and Ki means energy. Master Mikao Usui is the founder of Reiki.

In this healing method, the healer becomes the medium of universal healing energy and transfer the energy through hands or universal powerful energy symbols to the person to be healed. In doing this, the energy transmitted will neutralize the negative energy, disintegrate it , extract from the body energy field and increase the positive healing energy, positive thoughts, positive feelings and positive outlook of life. It is said it can alter the karmic impressions also.
